With a strong presence in Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Indore, the company ensures accessibility through offline agents, direct sales teams, a mobile app, and website.

- Domain: Finance/Banking (Insurance Data)

### ✅Problem Statement:

-	Client **‘Shield Insurance Ltd.’** currently lacks a consolidated & interactive view to effectively monitor customer growth, revenue performance, and policy trends.
-	Additionally, there is limited visibility into how customer behaviour varies across age groups, cities, sales modes, and policies. 
-	This makes it difficult for stakeholders to identify growth trends, evaluate sales channel performance, and understand demographic impacts on revenue and settlement expectations.
  
As an **Analyst**, our suggestion is:

- The organization will need to build and develop a modern end-to-end analytics reporting solution using Power BI to streamline data ingestion, transformation, and business reporting.

### ✅Project Objective:

-	Track major KPIs like ‘Total customers’ and ‘Total Revenue’
-	Monitor daily revenue growth rate & daily customer growth rate
-	Analyse month-over-month policy performance (Revenue vs Customer)
-	Customer Segmentation by ‘Age Group’ & ‘City’ to understand revenue and customer distribution
-	Enable dynamic comparison of revenue trends vs customer trends
-	Analyse age-group behaviour, including policy preference, sales mode preference, and average expected settlement
  
💡**This project demonstrates practical Data Analytics concepts**

- Establishing an end-to-end workflow for smooth analysis.
- Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
- Data modeling & establishing relationships 
- Data Visualization using KPI Cards, Charts, and many more
- Building a ‘business-ready’ finance analytics reporting using semantic models

### ✅Analytics Objective:

As an Analyst, I am responsible for developing and implementing a Power BI Model that will provide an end-to-end analytics solution designed to track and analyse ‘Revenue growth’, ‘Customer Behaviour’, ‘Sales Performance’, and ‘Age-Wise’ customer segmentation

### ➡️The Approach: 

-	**Data Architecture & ETL:** Extracted and loaded multi-source relational datasets into Power BI. Standardized text entries, handled missing keys, and structured date keys.
-	**Advanced Data Modeling:** Implemented a Star Schema by establishing relationships between central transaction tables (fact_premiums, fact_settlements) and master dimension tables (dim_customer, dim_date, dim_policy). Successfully identified and resolved complex model issues, including data blockages from one-way cross-filtering directions and circular dependencies.
-	**Advanced DAX Engineering:** Developed robust, custom DAX metrics for time-intelligence analysis. Designed string-concatenation formulas for dynamic card visual subtitles and established conditional color flags for automated positive/negative trend markers.
-	**UI/UX Dashboard Styling:** Designed left-side navigation matrix mimicking modern software applications. Integrated functional dashboard layout enhancements including conditional formatting data bars, custom line/column combinations for concurrent trend tracking, and user-driven metric trend switches.

### ✅ Key Insights:

1. **Global Revenue Operations**: Shield Insurance successfully generated a total revenue of 989.25M across 26,841 active customers, producing an average premium value of 36,856 per policyholder.
   
2. **Top Regional Market**: Delhi NCR stands as the ultimate revenue engine for the organization, generating 401.6M (more than 40% of total company revenue) from 11,007 customers.
   
3. **Secondary Regional Performance**: Mumbai forms the second-largest market cluster with 239.5M, followed closely by Hyderabad at 160.5M.
   
4. **Smaller Market Footprints**: Chennai and Indore represent the company's smaller regional footprints, generating 106.3M (2,966 customers) and 81.3M (2,096 customers) respectively.
   
5. **Dominant Sales Channel**: Traditional acquisition methods remain highly influential, with the Offline-Agent channel commanding a clear majority share of 55.7% of total revenue and 55.4% of all customers.
   
6. **Sales Mode** is led by the Online-App at 16.3% revenue share, which successfully outpaces website sales (Online-Website at 12.6%)
   
7. **Policy Product**: Policy ID POL2005HEL serves as the company's highest-grossing product, responsible for a staggering 324.26M in total revenue from just 1,968 customers.
   
8. **Core Target Demographic:** The 31–40 age cohort is the company's primary target audience, leading all customer brackets with 285.08M in generated revenue and 9,901 individual policyholders.
   
9. **Underperforming Youth Segment**: The youngest consumer bracket (18–25) exhibits the lowest market penetration with only 25.49M in total revenue and 1,860 customers.
  
10. **Claim Settlements**: Average claim settlement rates exhibit a direct positive correlation with age; the 65+ age demographic maintains the highest settlement rate at 72.2%, whereas the 18–25 demographic holds the lowest rate at 40.0%.
